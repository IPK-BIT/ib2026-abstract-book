Doreen Ware, Adjunct Professor at CSHL and USDA computational biologist, is a plant genomics expert. Her research uses advanced computational analysis and modeling to study crop genome evolution, standardize multi-omics data, and translate genomic information into strategies for improving plant range and yield. As a founding architect and principal investigator for infrastructures like Gramene and SorghumBase, she develops robust, open-access data environments for comparative genomics. Prof. Ware embeds FAIR data principles into large-scale biological databases, linking bioinformatics with sustainable agriculture.
