Dr. Can Türker is a leading database systems expert and the Head of Data Management at the Functional Genomics Center Zurich (FGCZ), a joint state-of-the-art research facility of ETH Zurich and the University of Zurich. With over 30 years of deep academic and industry experience specializing in extended and object-relational database systems, he is the author of several foundational textbooks in the field of data engineering. For the past two decades, Dr. Türker has applied this rigorous computer science expertise to the life sciences as the inventor and lead architect of B-Fabric. This unique, highly advanced data integration system is custom-built to tackle the complexities of massive Multi-OMICS research. B-Fabric seamlessly captures and links the entire lifecycle of biological data—from initial project design and physical sample tracking to instrument data generation and downstream computational analysis. By bridging the gap between hardcore database theory and practical bioinformatics, Dr. Türker’s pioneering infrastructure work exemplifies how robust, long-term system architectures can operationalize FAIR principles and enable reproducible, large-scale systems biology.
