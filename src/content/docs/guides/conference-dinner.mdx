Join us for an evening of local history, relaxation, and networking. The conference social program transitions smoothly from an afternoon guided tour of the historic town of Quedlinburg into our conference dinner.

---

## Evening Schedule

*   **18:00 – 19:15** | **Guided Historic City Tour**  
    Meet at the **City Tour Starting Point** (Marktplatz area). A local English-speaking guide will take us through the UNESCO World Heritage old town architecture. The walk ends directly at the dinner venue.
*   **19:30 – Late** | **Conference Dinner**  
    Doors open at the restaurant for drinks, followed shortly by the opening buffet.

---

## Venue & Location

The dinner takes place at the **Best Western Premier Schlossmühle** restaurant area, nestled right below the historic castle hill (*Schlossberg*) in Quedlinburg.

> 💳 **Cost:** Access to the evening city tour, full buffet dinner is entirely **included in your conference registration fee**. Please ensure you bring your conference badge for verification at the entrance.
